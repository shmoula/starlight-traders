before launch) and **launch week**. Hooks are written; just fill dates.

## Track A — Build-in-public devlogs (weeks T-4 → T-1)

Post each as a short devlog with a screenshot/GIF. Cross-post: r/incremental_games (where
allowed), r/gamedev (Feedback/Screenshot threads), X/Bluesky. The goal is _recruiting
playtesters and building a Discord_, not selling.

## Ongoing ritual (post-launch retention engine)

- **Daily:** auto/manual "Today's seed" post in Discord + X with yesterday's top 3 scores. This is the habit loop — protect it.
- **Weekly:** a balance/feature note. Turns players into co-authors.
- **Repurpose:** every interesting run (a comeback from near-bankruptcy, a record score) is a 15-second clip. Use the AI-accelerated pipeline: capture → caption → post 3 hook variants → double down on the winner.

---

## Cold-start rule (do NOT skip)

Saturate **ONE community first** (r/incremental_games + the Discord) before widening to
r/WebGames, indie newsletters, or paid. A dense, engaged subnetwork that actually talks to
each other beats thin spray across ten channels. Expand only after the daily-seed ritual is
visibly sticking (people posting scores unprompted).
